Anyone modifying the design should provide brief information about the modifications, including the date they were made, in this file.

Information about the design should be added to but never removed from this file.

According to section 3.3b of the CERN OHL license, licensees should provide a brief entry with a date and the nature of the modification for each design change. 

For example:

2022-01-16 - Alexander Curtiss apcurtiss@gmail.com

Added note to schematic regarding microphone incompatability with MCU.
